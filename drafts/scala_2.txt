Ok… Ok… You might ask, "But what if I'd like to override the getters, setters provided automatically or add new constructors?" Well, of course you can do that, really easily too.
You might have noticed that the syntax for declaring variables is reversed (you write x: Double instead of Double x like in Java). This is one of the most difficult aspects of Scala for newcomers to wrap their head around but for now it's enough to know that it is reversed for a reason.
